About the college

It is one of the oldest and most prestigious colleges in the region, serving as a major educational hub for students from rural areas.

Basic details

  • Establishment: 2 January 1970 — established by the Junnar Taluka Shivner Shikshan Prasarak Mandal.
  • Location: Bodkenagar, Junnar, Dist. Pune, Maharashtra — Pin Code 410502.
  • Affiliation: Permanently affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU).
  • Accreditation: NAAC B+; DST-FIST sponsored institution; Best College award by Pune University.
  • Campus: Lush green campus of about 10 acres.

Vision & mission

Vision: To impart quality higher education to rural youth for inclusive development.

Mission: To cultivate knowledge, skills and values through learner-centric pedagogy, research culture and community engagement.

Principal's message

We strive for academic rigour, discipline and character formation. Our portals connect students and staff to marks, attendance, materials and transparent admission workflows — reflecting our commitment to accountable, learner-centred governance.

History (summary)

From modest beginnings in 1970, the institution expanded infrastructure — main building, science block, central library, hostels, gymkhana and research centres — while deepening its engagement with the communities of Junnar taluka and beyond.
