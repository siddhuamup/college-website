Courses offered

The college offers more than 20 courses across Arts, Commerce, Science, and IT at Undergraduate (UG), Postgraduate (PG), and Doctoral (Ph.D.) levels. Below is the official catalogue summary; the live list from the admissions office loads when the portal server is running.

Undergraduate (UG — 3 years)

  • B.A. — History, Geography, English, Marathi, Hindi, Psychology, and allied subjects
  • B.Sc. — Chemistry, Physics, Botany, Mathematics, and related combinations
  • B.Com
  • BBA and BBA (Computer Applications)

Postgraduate (PG — 2 years)

  • M.A. — English, Geography, History
  • M.Sc. — Various specializations
  • M.Com — Advanced Accounting & Taxation; Banking & Finance

Ph.D. programmes

Recognized research centres for Ph.D. in Commerce and Ph.D. in Zoology, as per Savitribai Phule Pune University norms.
