Admission process

  1. Complete the online application on this page (connected to the college database).
  2. Upload supporting documents (PDF or image).
  3. The office verifies documents; merit-based lists follow SPPU norms.
  4. Track status anytime with your application number and email.

UG admissions are merit-based on 12th marks (Maharashtra Board, CBSE, or ISC).
