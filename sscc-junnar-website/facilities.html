Infrastructure & facilities

The campus is spread over a lush green area of about 10 acres at Bodkenagar, Junnar.

Library

Highly enriched central library with over 1,09,000 books, 14 daily newspapers, e-books, and reputed journals; dedicated reading hall and e-learning / computer section.

Sports complex

Multi-activity indoor sports stadium, gymnasium, and large outdoor playground. Table tennis, badminton, basketball, and wrestling are encouraged.

Hostel

Boys' hostel on campus with capacity of about 40 students; reading room and purified drinking water.

Laboratories

17 advanced, well-equipped laboratories for Science and Computer / IT students.

Other facilities

Primary medical health centre, two cafeterias / canteens, and strong IT infrastructure.
